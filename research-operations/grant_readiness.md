## Positioning

This track frames MHLM / MDLH as an AI-safety and scientific-reasoning audit project.
The goal is not to claim a confirmed physics discovery. The goal is to document,
compare, and test how different AI systems reason about complex scientific claims,
where their outputs converge, and where they diverge from experimentally grounded
constraints.
